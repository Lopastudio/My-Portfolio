About me

Hi, my name is Patrik and I am a highschool student from Bratislava. I got into programming at the age of 8 and I learned Python, JavaScript, Django, and web development at ITStep Academy (from 2017 all the way to 2023). I also like playing around with electronics components, soldering random circuits off the internet and stuff like that :)

I am also an amateur radio operator and I really like this hobby. You can talk to people from all around the world with just some wire hanging from a tree and a simple radio in your room. I also enjoy listening to shortwave radio, and recording weird / mysterious signals.

If you want to read more about me, you can do that on my older website (which I use as my blog now).
